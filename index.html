About Us

We have a team of engineers, researchers and scientists, covering all Technology Readiness Levels (TRLs) in the field of dynamics and tribology. We believe that the right balance of fundamental engineering science and applied engineering can find an effective and optimised solution for modern engineering challenges. In striking this balance, we utilise a smart combination of software tools and experimental techniques.

Learn More

Field of Activity

Our current products and services offer solutions for noise, vibration and harshness (NVH) and tribology of mechanical systems. This is delivered using a portfolio of software and hardware platforms, tackling multidisciplinary, multi scale and multi physics engineering problems. This holistic approach would not be possible only using standard codes, analytical methods or pure experiments.

Hardware Solutions

We have variety of bespoke and purpose-built test rigs for including:

  • Gear NVH and tribology test rigs (cylindrical, hypoid, spiral bevel and bevel gears)
  • Bearing and seals tribo-dynamic test rig
  • Wet and dry clutch test rig

Our flagship test rigs are marketed internationally by TriboDENS SWEEP in Europe and North America.

  • Fretting and reciprocating tribometers
  • Surface measurement machines (eg. FTIR, XPS, SEM and TEM)
  • Laser vibrometers
  • Exciters
  • Optical surface topography measurement
  • CMM (Coordinate Measuring Machine)
  • Profilometers

Our flagship high-speed and electrified bearing test rig, TriboDENS SWEEP, is marketed in Europe and North America.
