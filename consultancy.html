Consultancy

Extensive Experience Of Collaborative Industry Projects Informs Our Leading Position In Experimental And Software Innovations.

Wide-ranging consultancy capabilities include:

  • Hybrid powertrain NVH modelling and experimentation, and objective optimisation of electro-mechanical integration
  • Wear and friction characterisation for hard coatings
  • Transmission error characterisation for high performance hypoid gear pairs from measured profile
  • High speed bearing characterisation (stiffness and damping) for electrified systems
  • Optimum oil-additive package development for high performance powertrains
  • Wet clutch optimisation for efficiency and thermal management
  • Oil spray modelling and experimentation for high speed gears
  • DLC coating optimisation and validation for valvetrains
  • Surface texture design, optimisation and validation
  • Friction, wear and NVH simulation and validation for spiral bevel gears
  • NVH refinement of a high performance dual-clutch transmission
